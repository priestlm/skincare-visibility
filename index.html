    The shift is already happening

    Buyers moved to AI. Most brands haven't noticed.

    Shoppers used to start with Google. Now they open ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity or Google's AI Mode and ask a question like "what's the best jacket for a coastal walk?" — and the AI replies with a handful of specific brand recommendations.

    If your brand isn't in that answer, you don't get the click, the visit, or the sale. The buyer doesn't see your Google ranking. They see whoever AI recommended.

    This is different from SEO. SEO is about ranking in a list of links. AI visibility is about whether an AI model recommends your brand by name when a buyer asks for a recommendation. A brand can have a strong Google ranking and still be invisible to AI buyers.

    Common questions

    Everything you need to know

    What is Visible?

    Visible is an on-demand AI visibility reporting platform. We test whether your brand is being recommended by AI assistants when buyers ask product questions in your category. Generate a report whenever you want — each one shows where you appear, where you're missing, who appears instead, and what to do about it. Run reports regularly and your Tracker builds a history of how your visibility changes over time.

    What is AI visibility?

    AI visibility is whether your brand gets mentioned when someone asks an AI assistant for a product recommendation. It's different from your website's Google ranking — AI assistants don't show a list of links, they name specific brands. If your brand isn't named, you don't get the buyer's attention, regardless of your SEO ranking.

    Is this the same as SEO?

    No. SEO helps your website rank in search engine results pages. AI visibility is about whether an AI model recommends your brand by name when a buyer asks for a suggestion. A brand can have a first-page Google ranking and still be completely absent from AI recommendations. They require different content strategies.

    What do I get from the free preview?

    The free preview shows your business category and niche as detected from your website, five real buyer AI-search questions relevant to your brand, and the live AI response for each (click any question to see it). You also get initial visibility gap signals. It's a genuine early signal — but it's not a full report.

    What does the £29/month subscription include?

    Access to generate on-demand AI visibility reports for up to 5 websites. Each report includes ten sections: an AI visibility score, brand footprint audit, unbranded discovery score, all prompts tested, appeared and missed results, competitor intelligence, gaps by intent type, five priority actions, and a full evidence appendix showing every query and AI response. Every report is saved to your Tracker — run them regularly and you build a history of how your visibility changes over time.

    How often can I generate a report?

    Whenever you want. There is no fixed schedule — you log into your Tracker and run a report for any of your saved websites at any time. The first report is your baseline. From the second report onwards your Tracker shows score change, queries that improved, and queries that regressed. The more often you run reports, the richer your visibility history becomes.

    What happens after I enter my website URL?

    We read your public website to detect your business category, niche, products and market. We then generate relevant buyer questions and show you a free preview within a few seconds. If we can't read your website (some larger sites block automated access), we ask you to select your category manually and still generate a preview.

With Visibility Tracker, we'll carry out a manual review of your public pages as part of the service.

    Do I need to install anything?

    No. Visible is entirely web-based. You enter a URL, we do the rest. No plugins, no tracking code, no access to your website backend, no Google Analytics integration required. Nothing is installed on your site.

We aim to make cancellation as simple as sending an email.

    Which types of businesses is this for?

    Visible is built for consumer-facing brands, local services and product-led businesses where AI recommendations influence buyer choice — food and drink, fashion, beauty, homeware, wellness, fitness, pets, baby products, local trades and professional services. If a buyer might ask an AI "what's the best X?" or "where should I go for Y?" in your category, Visible is relevant to you. It's less suited to purely B2B businesses, wholesale, or commodity markets where buyers don't typically rely on AI for brand recommendations.

    Do you guarantee AI rankings?

    No. No one can guarantee AI rankings — AI models are trained independently, update regularly, and respond differently to different prompt wordings. Visible gives you an honest, evidence-based view of your current AI visibility and practical guidance on how to improve it. The improvements we recommend are based on Google's own AI Optimisation guidance and direct content analysis — not SEO tricks.

    Is Visible affiliated with ChatGPT, Google, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity or other AI providers?

    No. Visible is entirely independent. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any AI provider. We use AI language models as tools to run tests — the same way a buyer would use them. Our findings and recommendations are our own. We identify which AI provider we used in each report's evidence appendix.

    Why does it matter which queries I appear in?

    Not all missed queries are equal. A buyer asking "best [product] for [specific use case]" is much more commercially valuable than one asking a broad category question. Visibility Tracker groups missed queries by commercial relevance and intent type — so you know which gaps actually cost you sales and which are lower priority.

    What's inside every Visibility Tracker report?

    Every report covers ten sections in the same format: (1) AI Visibility Score — a 0–100 score of how often your brand appeared across all tested prompts; (2) AI Brand Footprint — five direct questions to the AI about your brand's recognition, products, and stockist knowledge; (3) Unbranded Discovery Score — whether AI recommends you when buyers ask category questions without naming you; (4) All Prompts Tested — every buyer question run, grouped by intent type; (5) Appeared & Missed Results — where you showed up and where you didn't, graded by commercial relevance; (6) Competitor Intelligence — which brands AI recommended instead, by confidence level; (7) Gaps by Customer Intent — whether gaps are in discovery, comparison, local or product-specific queries; (8) Priority Actions — five specific ranked actions linked to missed queries; (9) Progress Tracking — score changes and query-level trends from your second report onwards; (10) Full Evidence Appendix — every query tested, the complete AI response, and the confidence level assigned. Nothing is summarised away.

    How does Visible generate and test AI queries?

    We analyse your public website to understand your category, niche, products and target buyer. We then generate 20+ buyer questions a real customer might ask an AI assistant — focused on unbranded discovery ("what's the best X for Y?") rather than direct brand lookups. Each question is submitted live to an AI language model. We record the full response, which brands were recommended, the confidence level (based on how many recommendations the AI gave), and whether your brand appeared. We use one AI provider per report, clearly identified in the evidence appendix.

    Separately, we ask the AI five direct questions about your brand — recognition, product knowledge, stockist awareness, comparison positioning, and retail availability. This tells us whether AI knows your brand at all, separate from whether it recommends you unprompted. Your visibility score counts only queries with reliable AI responses (4+ recommendations). We are not affiliated with any AI provider.

    How is AI visibility different from traditional SEO?

    SEO helps your website rank in search engine results. AI visibility is about whether an AI assistant names your brand when a buyer asks for a recommendation — no link list, no ranking page. A brand can have strong SEO and still be invisible to AI. The differences run deeper: SEO optimises for keywords and page authority; AI visibility depends on buyer intent, conversational context, and whether your brand is present in the information AI has been trained on or can access. Both matter. SEO remains important. AI visibility is an emerging layer on top — and one most brands are not yet measuring.
