    The shift is already happening

    Buyers moved to AI. Most brands haven't noticed.

    Shoppers used to start with Google. Now they open ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity or Google's AI Mode and ask a question like "what's the best jacket for a coastal walk?" — and the AI replies with a handful of specific brand recommendations.

    If your brand isn't in that answer, you don't get the click, the visit, or the sale. The buyer doesn't see your Google ranking. They see whoever AI recommended.

    This is different from SEO. SEO is about ranking in a list of links. AI visibility is about whether an AI model recommends your brand by name when a buyer asks for a recommendation. A brand can have a strong Google ranking and still be invisible to AI buyers.

    Methodology

    How we test. What we measure. What we don't claim.

    How we generate questions

    We analyse your public website to understand your category, niche, products and target buyer. We then generate 20+ buyer questions that a real customer might type into an AI assistant — focused on unbranded discovery ("what's the best X for Y?") not brand lookups ("tell me about brand Z").

    How we test

    Each question is submitted live to an AI language model. We record the full response, which brands were recommended, the confidence level of the result (based on how many recommendations the AI gave), and whether your brand appeared. We use one AI provider per report, clearly identified in the evidence appendix.

    Brand footprint audit

    Separately from discovery queries, we ask the AI five direct questions about your brand: recognition, product knowledge, stockist awareness, comparison positioning, and retail availability. This tells us whether AI knows your brand at all — separate from whether it recommends you unprompted.

    Scoring and limitations

    Your visibility score counts only queries with reliable AI responses (4+ recommendations). Thin or empty responses are excluded from the score but shown in the evidence appendix. AI outputs change as models update — single-month results are baselines, not conclusions. We do not guarantee AI rankings and are not affiliated with any AI provider.

    Common questions

    Everything you need to know

    What is Visible?

    Visible is a monthly AI visibility reporting service. We test whether your brand is being recommended by AI assistants when buyers ask product questions in your category. Each month you receive a written report showing where you appear, where you're missing, who appears instead, and what to do about it.

    What is AI visibility?

    AI visibility is whether your brand gets mentioned when someone asks an AI assistant for a product recommendation. It's different from your website's Google ranking — AI assistants don't show a list of links, they name specific brands. If your brand isn't named, you don't get the buyer's attention, regardless of your SEO ranking.

    Is this the same as SEO?

    No. SEO helps your website rank in search engine results pages. AI visibility is about whether an AI model recommends your brand by name when a buyer asks for a suggestion. A brand can have a first-page Google ranking and still be completely absent from AI recommendations. They require different content strategies.

    What do I get from the free preview?

    The free preview shows your business category and niche as detected from your website, five real buyer AI-search questions relevant to your brand, and the live AI response for each (click any question to see it). You also get initial visibility gap signals. It's a genuine early signal — but it's not a full report.

    What does the £29/month Starter Report include?

    Ten sections: an AI visibility score, brand footprint audit, unbranded discovery score, all prompts tested, appeared and missed results, competitor intelligence, gaps by intent type, five priority actions, month-on-month tracking, and a full evidence appendix showing every query and AI response. Delivered by email each month.

    How often is the report updated?

    Monthly. We re-run the same buyer prompts each month so you can track whether your visibility is improving. The first report is your baseline. Progress becomes clear from the second report onwards when we show score change, queries that improved, and queries that regressed.

    What happens after I enter my website URL?

    We read your public website to detect your business category, niche, products and market. We then generate relevant buyer questions and show you a free preview within a few seconds. If we can't read your website (some larger sites block automated access), we ask you to select your category manually and still generate a preview.

    What happens if my website can't be read?

    This can happen with sites that use JavaScript rendering, bot protection, or are hosted on platforms that block external requests. If we can't read your site automatically, we ask you to select your business category manually. The free preview still works. For the full Starter Report, we'll carry out a manual review of your public pages as part of the service.

    Do I need to install anything?

    No. Visible is entirely web-based. You enter a URL, we do the rest. No plugins, no tracking code, no access to your website backend, no Google Analytics integration required. Nothing is installed on your site.

    Can I cancel?

    Yes, at any time. There is no minimum contract. Email us to cancel and we will stop the next month's charge immediately. We don't offer partial-month refunds, but you'll keep access to all reports you've already received. We aim to make cancellation as simple as sending an email.

    Which types of businesses is this for?

    Visible is designed for small and medium-sized brands that sell products or services to consumers — food, drink, clothing, beauty, homeware, wellness, fitness, pets, baby products, local services, and professional services. It's most useful for brands that want to know how AI perceives them relative to competitors. It's less relevant for purely B2B businesses or commodities with no brand differentiation.

    Do you guarantee AI rankings?

    No. No one can guarantee AI rankings — AI models are trained independently, update regularly, and respond differently to different prompt wordings. Visible gives you an honest, evidence-based view of your current AI visibility and practical guidance on how to improve it. The improvements we recommend are based on Google's own AI Optimisation guidance and direct content analysis — not SEO tricks.

    Is Visible affiliated with ChatGPT, Google, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity or other AI providers?

    No. Visible is entirely independent. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any AI provider. We use AI language models as tools to run tests — the same way a buyer would use them. Our findings and recommendations are our own. We identify which AI provider we used in each report's evidence appendix.

    Why does it matter which queries I appear in?

    Not all missed queries are equal. A buyer asking "best [product] for [specific use case]" is much more commercially valuable than one asking a broad category question. The Starter Report groups missed queries by commercial relevance and intent type — so you know which gaps actually cost you sales and which are lower priority.
