    Methodology

    How we test. What we measure. What we don't claim.

    How we test

    We generate 20+ realistic buyer questions tailored to your specific business — the kind a real customer would type into ChatGPT or a similar AI assistant. We submit each question live to an AI language model and record whether your brand appeared in the response.

    Brand footprint audit

    Separately from the discovery queries, we ask the AI five direct questions about your brand: whether it recognises the name, what products it associates with you, and where it believes customers can buy. This gives a full picture of AI's current understanding of your brand.

    Scoring

    Your visibility score is the percentage of reliable results (responses with 4–5 recommendations) where your brand appeared. Results with fewer than 2 recommendations are excluded from the score but shown in the evidence appendix.

    Limitations

    AI model outputs change as models update, as new training data is incorporated, and as competitors improve their content. Results on any given day are directional, not definitive. Single-month reports are baselines — meaningful trends emerge from comparing multiple months.
