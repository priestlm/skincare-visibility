Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Visible ("we", "us", "our") is committed to being transparent about how we handle your information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, when it may be shared with third-party providers to run the analysis, and your rights.

Who we are

Visible is an early-stage SaaS product providing on-demand AI visibility analysis for small businesses under the Visibility Tracker product. We are based in the United Kingdom. You can contact us at mark@visibleai.co.uk.

What information we collect

  • Website URLs and brand details — submitted by you when running a free preview or generating a report. Up to 5 websites or brands can be saved to your account. These are the primary inputs to the analysis.
  • Business category and market information — detected from your public website or selected by you manually. Used to generate relevant buyer queries for your category.
  • Website-derived content — publicly available content read from your submitted URL (page text, metadata, product descriptions). Used to understand your brand and generate analysis inputs. We do not access any non-public areas of your website.
  • Report history and visibility data — each report you generate is saved to your account. This includes your visibility score, prompts tested, brand appearances, competitor data, gaps and recommended actions. Saved history allows you to track changes over time.
  • Email address — collected when you create an account or contact us. Used to authenticate your account, deliver results and respond to enquiries.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by our payment processor. We do not store your card details.
  • Usage data — basic logs of pages accessed and when, used to keep the service running and diagnose issues.

We do not collect personal data about your end customers or website visitors. Please do not submit confidential, commercially sensitive, or personal data belonging to third parties that you would not want processed by external AI or search services.

How we use your information

  • To generate AI visibility previews and on-demand reports for the websites you submit.
  • To save report history to your account so you can track your brand's AI visibility over time.
  • To enrich your visibility dataset with each report run, making trend analysis more meaningful.
  • To communicate about your account, subscription and support requests.
  • To improve the product based on aggregate, anonymised usage patterns.

We do not sell your data to third parties and we do not use your data for advertising.

AI analysis and third-party providers

Generating an AI visibility report requires submitting queries to external AI language models and public search tools. To do this, the following information may be sent to selected third-party AI and search providers:

  • Your submitted website URL and brand name
  • Your business category and market (e.g. "UK homeware brand")
  • Buyer queries generated from your website content
  • Publicly available content derived from your submitted URL

This processing is necessary to run the analysis. We identify which AI provider was used in each report's evidence appendix. These providers operate under their own data and usage policies — we select providers that offer appropriate terms for this type of use, but we do not control how those providers handle inputs on their infrastructure.

We are entirely independent from all AI providers and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Perplexity or any other AI platform.

AI-generated results are directional. They represent a snapshot of how AI systems responded to tested queries at the time each report was generated. Results may vary between runs due to the probabilistic nature of AI models and regular model updates. They do not constitute a guarantee of how any AI system will respond in future.

Data storage and retention

Your account data, saved websites, report history and visibility data are stored securely in our cloud infrastructure. Report history is retained while your account is active to support trend tracking — this is a core feature of the service.

If you cancel your subscription and request deletion of your data, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised or aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for product improvement purposes.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, email us at mark@visibleai.co.uk and we will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to keep you logged in and to maintain session state. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active subscribers by email of any material changes. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
